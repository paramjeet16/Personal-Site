How to make India Tea (Chai)

by Paramjeet Singh

Step 1

Pour 3/4 cups of water into a pot and let it boil


Step 2

Put one spoon of tea leaves and 1 spoon of sugar, then let it boil for 2 minutes


Step 3

Pour 1/2 cup of milk


Step 4

Let it boil for atleast 5 minutes


Step 5

Pour the tea into a cup through a strainer, and enjoy a hot evening Chai!
